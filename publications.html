Publications & Research

My contributions to peer-reviewed research in mechanical engineering and hydropower systems.

First Author

Investigation of Mechanical Properties of Brass Francis Turbine Manufactured by Local Investment Casting Technique in Nepal

Authors: Sangit Kattel (Lead Author), J. P. Bhatt, R. Subedi, B. Thapa, S. Sujakhu, A. Kafle, T. M. Shakya

Publication: Journal of Physics Conference Series

Volume & Issue: Volume 1608, Issue 1, Article 012013

Year: 2020

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1088/1742-6596/1608/1/012013

Abstract

This research investigates the mechanical properties of brass Francis turbine runners manufactured using the local investment casting technique in Nepal. Francis turbines are critical components in hydropower systems, and understanding the mechanical properties of locally-manufactured turbines is essential for ensuring system reliability and performance. The study evaluated tensile strength, hardness, and microstructural characteristics of brass turbines produced locally, comparing them against international standards.

Key Findings

  • Characterized mechanical properties of locally-cast brass Francis turbine runners
  • Evaluated microstructural properties through detailed analysis
  • Assessed compliance with international turbine manufacturing standards
  • Provided insights into the feasibility of local turbine manufacturing in Nepal

Significance

This research contributes to the development of cost-effective, locally-manufactured hydropower components for Nepal. By validating the properties of locally-cast turbines, the study supports the development of Nepal's hydropower industry and reduces dependency on expensive imported components.

My Contribution

As the first author, I led the research design, conducted the experimental testing, performed materials analysis, and wrote the primary manuscript. This paper resulted from my undergraduate project and represents my first significant contribution to peer-reviewed engineering literature.

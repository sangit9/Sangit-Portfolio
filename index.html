About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a Bachelor of Engineering (Hydropower Specialisation) from Kathmandu University and a Master of Engineering from the Engineering Institute of Technology, Melbourne. I have been formally assessed by Engineers Australia as a Professional Mechanical Engineer. My expertise includes hydromechanical design, computational fluid dynamics (CFD) analysis, non-destructive testing (NDT), mechanical inspection, and engineering analysis for hydropower projects. Through my academic background and industry experience, I have developed strong technical and problem-solving skills in the design, assessment, and maintenance of mechanical systems. Now based in Adelaide, South Australia, I am actively seeking opportunities as a Mechanical Engineer where I can contribute my technical expertise, continue to develop professionally, and add value to innovative engineering projects.
